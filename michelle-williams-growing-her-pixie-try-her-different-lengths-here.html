Ever since debuting a pixie in spring of 2010, Michelle Williams hasn't looked back at her Dawson's-era hairstyles—until now! The star, who is one-third of our March cover, revealed that growing out her hair is currently at the top of her to-do list. "I think I'm ready! It's on its way, slowly but surely," Williams told E!
