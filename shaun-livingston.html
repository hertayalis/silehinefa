Shaun Livingston (born September 11, 1985) is an American Former Basketball Player based in Peoria (Illinois, USA). He played some league matches of his High School on the point guard position Then, he drafted for the NBA after his high school graduation.

He signed by the Los Angeles Clippers in 2004 draft. However, he injured the left part of his knee in 2017. Livingston took around two years to recover from his injury.

Later on, he selected in the teams’ Oklahoma City Thunder, Washington Wizards, Brooklyn Nets and Miami Heat. Shaun won three Golden State Warriors in 2015, 2017 and 2018.

Still, he announced his retirement from NBA after 15 years. He gave an honest contribution to the sport. On the other hand, he dropped his college education for his first NBA.

Profile, Parents & Life Before Fame

Shaun Patrick Livingston was born on 11th of September 1985 in Peoria (Illinois, USA). He is the talented child of his mom Ann Wyman and dad Reggie Livingston. Furthermore, he is the only brother of his sisters Sarah Aupperle-Livingston and Blair Wyman.

He received his enrollment in the Concordia Lutheran Grade School. Then, he polished his basketball player skills at Richwoods High School. He transferred after two years to the Peoria Central High School.

Additionally, he led his teammates in the ClassAA state titles in 2003-2004. He named as the Illinois Mr. Basketball. He got the chance and played in the McDonalds’ All American. Moreover, he voted in the 100 Legends of the IHSA Boys Basketball Tournament.

Spouse, Wedding, Children & Love

In 2017, Shaun and Joanna Williams happily became life partners. They have become the proud mother-father of two babies Jonah Jazmine Livingston and Tyler Williams. His current residence located in Oakland, California.
